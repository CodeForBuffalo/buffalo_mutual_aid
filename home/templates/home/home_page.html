Buffalo Mutual Aid Network

Buffalo Mutual Aid Network is a social action group for peer-to-peer organizing, humanitarian assistance, and reliable information sharing developed in response to the COVID-19 crisis.

Offer help Request help

Help provide aid

Consider volunteering to provide mutual aid during this crisis. This can include delivering groceries or supplies. You might have another skill or resource to contribute, such as providing childcare. Compensation for your transportation, time, or service might be available.

Sign up to provide aid

Donate to fund volunteer work

All money raised will go towards mutual aid, which will include providing food, over the counter medicine and cleaning supplies. We are preparing to include direct support such as childcare, care giving, and running errands. This is not charity, this is solidarity and the truest sense of community. Please donate now to support these efforts!

Donate on GoFundMe

Provide childcare

We help pair childcare providers with families based on everyone's needs. We aim to keep the needs of the childcare provider and the families in the best interest that we can. We ask for a reference as a way to guarantee safe care.

Apply to provide childcare


Work In Progress

Buffalo Mutual Aid Network is an evolving community effort managed entirely by volunteers. This website is an ongoing project with Code for Buffalo volunteers.

If you'd like to join us in helping lead this collective effort, welcome!
